Our Services

At First Class Finishing LLC, we are dedicated to transforming spaces with unmatched craftsmanship and attention to detail. Whether you’re planning a large-scale renovation or a small update, our expert team is here to guide you through every step, ensuring your project is completed with precision, care, and a focus on your unique needs.

Contracting Services

Our contracting services provide end-to-end project management, ensuring your vision becomes reality without the stress. From the initial consultation, where we assess your goals and budget, to detailed planning and execution, we take care of every aspect of the job. We coordinate with trusted subcontractors, source the highest-quality materials, and keep you informed every step of the way. Choosing the right contractor is critical to the success of your project. With First Class Finishing, you’re guaranteed professionalism, transparency, and results that exceed expectations.

Commercial Painting

First impressions matter, especially in business. Our commercial painting services are designed to create a professional, polished look that reflects your brand and leaves a lasting impression on clients and employees. We start by discussing your goals and conducting a thorough assessment of your space. Our team prepares surfaces meticulously, ensuring a smooth and long-lasting finish. With minimal disruption to your daily operations, we work efficiently to complete your project on time and to the highest standards. Investing in professional commercial painting ensures your workspace is both functional and visually appealing.

Residential Painting

Your home deserves the best care, and our residential painting services deliver just that. We begin by helping you choose the perfect color scheme to complement your home’s style and atmosphere. From there, our skilled painters prepare your walls, ceilings, or exteriors with meticulous attention to detail, ensuring every surface is smooth and ready for painting. During the process, we protect your belongings and maintain a clean workspace, so you can enjoy peace of mind. A professional paint job not only enhances your home’s beauty but also adds lasting value and protection.

Bathtub and Shower Remodeling

A beautifully remodeled bathroom can transform your daily routine and add significant value to your home. Our bathtub and shower remodeling services begin with a consultation to understand your style, needs, and budget. Whether you’re looking to modernize an outdated space or create a luxurious retreat, we design and install solutions tailored to you. We focus on precision, using only high-quality materials and expert craftsmanship to ensure your new bathroom is both functional and stunning. Choosing an experienced remodeling team ensures a smooth process and a result you’ll love for years to come.
